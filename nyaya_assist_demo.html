⚡ BNS §103 Argument Builder
EXTRACTED FROM 5 MATCHING JUDGMENTS
▲ WINNING ARGUMENTS (use these)
→ Exhibit revenue records and survey sketches to establish motive chain conclusively — courts treat documentary motive proof as equivalent to direct evidence (Basavaraj, 2023).
→ Produce minimum two independent, credible witnesses. Government servants and healthcare workers have been consistently treated as high-credibility PWs (Rajanna, 2020).
→ Argue non-recovery of weapon as consistent with accused's guilt — distinguish from innocence using flight pattern and last-seen evidence.
▼ LOSING PATTERNS (avoid / remedy)
→ Never file without a magistrate-recorded dying declaration. SI-recorded statements have been rejected in 3 of 5 similar Karnataka cases. (Krishnappa, 2021)
→ Close all identified but unexamined witnesses before filing. Hostile PW-3 in Govindaswamy (2019) collapsed the entire prosecution case despite strong circumstantial evidence.
→ File within 60 days. BNSS §193 deadline breach results in bail by default regardless of evidence quality. (State v. Prakash, Mysuru, 2022)
